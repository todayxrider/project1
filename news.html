Lung Cancer Research

On May 8, 2015

The Massachusets General Hospital invites you to join us for Honky Tonk Woman event.

Lung Cancer Event

The Northwestern Medicine Lung Cancer Advisory Council invites you to join us for Honky Tonk Woman, a lively evening of camaraderie and celebration as we pay tribute to all lung cancer patients and, in particular, the late Diane Cole. This special event will include heavy hors d’oeuvres and cocktails, a live DJ, dancing to 60s classics and a silent auction; club attire is appropriate. All proceeds will support lung cancer research through the Lung Cancer Tissue Bank at Northwestern Medicine.

Neurological Surgery

On May 16, 2015

James P. Chandler, MD, was formally invested as the Lavin/Fates Professor of Neurological Surgery on Friday, May 16

About Dr. James Chandler

James P. Chandler, MD, was formally invested as the Lavin/Fates Professor of Neurological Surgery on Friday, May 16. Dr. Chandler is co-director and surgical director of the Northwestern Brain Tumor Institute (NBTI), and is the Rudy E. Sabbagha, MD, Distinguished Physician in Surgical Neuro-Oncology at Northwestern Memorial Hospital. He has performed over 3,500 successful brain tumor operations in his career.
