Cardiac Surgery

Massachussets General Hospital’s team of cardiac surgeons specializes in treating high-risk patients. South City Hospital Pvt Ltd have also achieved success in repairing the abnormal mitral valves rather than replacing them. This is made possible because of advances in surgical techniques and knowledge of mitral valve pathophysiology..

Angioplasty-Reason for the Procedure
  1. Greatly increases in blood flow.
  2. knee replacement Decrease pain.
  3. Increases ability for physical activity.
  4. Reduces risk of a heart attack.
Bypass Surgery-Reason for the Procedure
  1. Improves supply of bl/oxy to heart.
  2. Relieves chest pain (angina).
  3. Reduces risk of heart attack.
  4. Improves ability for physical activity.
Stent Surgery-Reason for the Procedure
  1. Holds the artery open.
  2. Improves blood flow to heart muscle.
  3. Relieves chest pain (angina).
  4. Improves Blodd Circulation.
