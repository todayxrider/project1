Who we are

We believe that quality health care should be affordable and accessible for all ages. This is why we bring you Proffessionals Emergency Doctors, a high-quality, cost-effective care alternative to meet your unscheduled medical needs..

Our team of Board Certified Emergency Medicine Physicians and medical staff are prepared to treat major medical conditions, as well as minor urgent care needs. Our facility is equipped with 18 examination rooms and on-site diagnostic equipment..
